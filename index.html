About Me

I am a postdoctoral researcher working at the intersection of AI, statistical genetics, and computational neuroscience. My research focuses on developing interpretable and carefully tailored multimodal methods to study complex psychiatric and neurological disorders, with the aim of translating methodological advances into meaningful scientific and clinical insight.

Longitudinal temperament trajectories
